🔴 A. Cach phat am /t/ 🔊 Nghe
/t/
/tiː/
  • Buoc 1: Dau luoi cham vao loi rang tren.
  • Buoc 2: Ha luoi xuong, hoi bat ra.
  • Giay truoc mom → rung manh.
  • Co hong khong rung (vo thanh).
  • 🎯 Target: /tə/

📌 Cach phat am /t/:

1. Dau luoi cham vao alveolar ridge (loi rang tren phia sau rang).

2. Ngan hoi lai hoan toan, sau do ha luoi, hoi bung ra.

3. Co hong khong rung — am vo thanh (voiceless).

🇳🇻 Tuong tu "t" tieng Viet nhung hoi thoi manh hon khi dung dau tu nhan.

🔵 C. Cach phat am /d/ 🔊 Nghe
/d/
/diː/
  • Buoc 1: Dau luoi cham vao loi rang tren.
  • Buoc 2: Ha luoi xuong, hoi thoat ra.
  • Giay truoc mom khong rung.
  • Co hong rung (huu thanh).
  • 🎯 Target: /də/

📌 Khac biet /t/ vs /d/:

1. /d/ — giay truoc mom khong rung (it hoi thoi).

2. /d/ co rung co hong (voicing) — dat tay len co cam nhan.

3. Ca hai deu la alveolar stop (chan hoi bang dau luoi + loi rang).

🇳🇻 Tuong tu "d" tieng Viet nhung bat loi rang ro hon.

🎯 Meo phan biet /t/ vs /d/

Test 1 — to giay: Dat to giay mong truoc mom.
→ /t/: giay rung manh    → /d/: giay gan nhu khong rung

Test 2 — tay len co:
→ /t/: khong rung    → /d/: cam thay rung (vibration)

Test 3 — minimal pairs: ten/den, tip/dip, tie/die, time/dime, town/down

🔊 Important for listening: /t/ va /d/ cuoi tu

Doi khi ban khong nghe ro /t/ hoac /d/ o cuoi tu. Nghe su khac biet:

something to eat → something to eat(t)
turn on the light → turn on the ligh(t)
the end of the road → the end of the roa(d)
writing on the board → writing on the boar(d)

▶ Nguyen am dai hon truoc /d/ hon truoc /t/: road vs wrOte  |  board vs bought

▶ /t/ hoac /d/ giua 2 phu am thuong bien mat: facts = fax, next week = nex(t) week

🎯 5 bi quyet phat am /t/ va /d/ chuan

1. Vi tri dau luoi chinh xac
Dau luoi cham vao alveolar ridge (khong phai rang, khong phai vom hong)
/tə/ — /də/ nhac lai nhieu lan de quen vi tri

2. /t/ cuoi tu — dung nuot am
"what, street, night, light" — phai bong mat du / bung hoi ro
/wɒt/ /striːt/ /naɪt/ /laɪt/

3. /d/ cuoi tu — nguyen am dai hon
"road" vs "wrote" — "road" nguyen am dai hon vi /d/ huu thanh
/rəʊd/ dai hon /rəʊt/

4. ed qua khu: /t/, /d/, /ɪd/ khac nhau
• /t/ sau vo thanh: stopped, washed, talked
• /d/ sau huu thanh: played, opened, called
• /ɪd/ sau /t/, /d/: wanted, needed, landed

5. Luyen cap toi thieu (minimal pairs)
ten/den, tip/dip, tie/die, town/down, wrote/road, light/lied

📝 Exercise 12.1 — Nghe va dien tu Bai tap sach

Nghe va dien tu thich hop vao cho trong. Chu y su khac biet /t/ va /d/ o cuoi tu.

🔊 Nhan nghe cau → Gõ tu vao o trong → Nhan "Cham bai".
🇳🇻 Vi du: What shall we do next week? → dien "next"
📝 Exercise 12.2 — Nghe va viet cac so Bai tap sach

Nghe va viet tu tuong ung voi tu trang thai ban nghe duoc.

🔊 Nhan nghe tung tu → Gõ tu bien the (vi du: send → sent, wide → whide) → Cham bai.
🇳🇻 Cac cap tu co su khac biet /t/ va /d/ o cuoi.
📝 Exercise 12.3 — Dien tu vao cau Bai tap sach

Nghe va dien tu thich hop co am /t/ hoac /d/ vao cho trong.

🔊 Nhan nghe → Gõ tu vao o trong → Cham bai.
📝 Exercise 12.4 — Nghe va doc bai tho 🔊 Co audio

Nghe va doc to cac bai tho sau. Chu y cac am /t/ (do) va /d/ (xanh).

🔴 Too many twos
Tom and Tim were twins.
Tom said to Tim, 'Can I talk to you?'
Tim said to Tom, 'Ssh, wait a minute ...'
One two is two
Two twos are four
Three twos are six
Four twos are eight
Five twos are ten ...'
Tom said to Tim, 'And what are two fives?'
Tim said to Tom, 'Two fives? Don't ask me!'
🔵 A difficult daughter
Doctor Dixon said to his daughter Daria,
'Don't go down town after dark — it's dangerous.'
Daria said, 'Don't worry, Dad, I won't. You know I never do.'
Next day when he came home for dinner, he said,
'Daria, dear, you didn't go down town after dark, did you?'
And she said, 'No, Dad, I didn't.'
But she did.
I don't know the details, but she definitely did.

🔊 Nhan nut Nghe → Chon tu dung → Cham bai.
